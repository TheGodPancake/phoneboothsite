FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Phonebooth

How do I add Phonebooth to my server?
Click the "Add to Server" button and follow the Discord authorization process. Make sure you have administrative permissions on the server you want to add it to.
Is Phonebooth safe to use?
Yes! Phonebooth includes robust reporting and moderation tools. We take user safety seriously and continuously monitor for inappropriate behavior.
How does cross-server communication work?
Phonebooth connects your server to our global network, allowing users to chat with people from other Discord servers in real-time while staying in their current server. It uses webhooks to make it appear like other users are actually in your chat, creating a seamless conversation experience.
Can I customize the bot's settings?
Absolutely! Use the 'c. config' command to enable what channels you want to use for Phonebooth.
