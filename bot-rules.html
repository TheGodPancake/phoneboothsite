Phonebooth Bot Rules

Follow these rules to maintain a safe and positive environment

Fundamental Rules

  • Please open a ticket If you are having an issue regarding the bot, do not dm staff.
  • NO NSFW topics: Strictly prohibited.
  • NO nsfw usernames or profiles: This goes back to our no NSFW rule we are strictly sfw and will not allow any accounts with suggestive, nsfw, inappropriate or offensive usernames/profiles on our bot.
  • Safety first: No flirting or "E-Dating" due to Discord's 13+ age requirement.
  • NO mentions or discussions of illegal/offensive activity: This includes drug abuse/activity, theft, substance use, piracy and everything alike.
  • Personal info: Do not share personal information, including your location or phone number.
  • Racism, homophobia, ableism, transphobia & derogatory behavior is not tolerated here & will lead to a instant ban.

Conduct Guidelines

  • Mutual respect: Please remember to treat others how you want to be treated, do not engage in bullying, hate speech or coordinating server raids to target a specific person or community.
  • Avoid controversial or sensitive topics: Certain topics such as religious beliefs, politics, war, violence, death, self harm and mental illness.
  • No toxicity: We like to avoid mellow dramatic drama or any toxicity period. This assures everyone stays happy.
  • No spamming: This includes spam pinging.
  • No sending harmful links and doxxing: Sending harmful links/phishing links, doxing and discussion of doing so is prohibited and will result in an immediate ban.
  • False reports: Do not falsely report users to ensure effective rule enforcement.
  • Invite links: No Discord invite links in chat; use DMs if requested.
  • Self-harm/suicide: Mentions of self-harm & suicide are forbidden to maintain a safe and happy environment.
  • Follow Discord guidelines: Adhere to Discord's guidelines and TOS to avoid being banned.

Strike System

  • If a user is reported for bad behavior on the bot, they will receive a warning strike via DM detailing the reason for the strike.
  • Accumulating three warning strikes will result in an automatic ban from the bot.
  • In cases of extreme offenses, the strike system may be bypassed, leading to an immediate ban without prior warning strikes.
  • Warning strikes may be removed at the discretion of a moderator.
